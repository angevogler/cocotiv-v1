ABOUT

CoCoTiv is a Coworking space that allows for energetic productivity in a social, uplifting, and "very cool" environment. The space will provide opportunities for meaningful connections and will inspire achievement and social collaboration. In this positive environment, individuals will be encouraged and will have the flexibility to work alone or together in a collaborative teams. Natural materials and a green wall will be utilized to provide a strong connection to nature. Bright colors will flow throughout the interior to inspire and motivate. A positive energy will be evident upon entering the space and will be carried throughout the interior.
